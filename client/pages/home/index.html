The North Shore .Net user group

was formed to provide a local platform for information sharing among developers in the Duluth/Superior area. The group meets the second Thursday of each month to learn about and discuss a wide variety of topics related to developement.

We are open to discussing any programming language, tool chains, developement patterns, etc.

If your interested in attending a meeting feel free to come and check it out. Join our MeetUp group for meeting remiders.
