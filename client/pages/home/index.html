The Duluth .NET User Group was formed to provide a local platform for information sharing among .NET developers in the Duluth/Superior area. The group meets the second Thursday of each month to learn about and discuss a wide variety of development related topics.

Meeting Format

  • 6:30 - 6:45 Mixer, food & drinks
  • 6:45 – 7:00 Housekeeping & announcements
  • 7:00 – 8:00 Event
  • 8:00 – 8:30 Break & peer networking
